# Design Research Brief

Reverse-engineer world-class enterprise products and synthesize one coherent operational design language for TravelOps AI Worker.

The product is an enterprise agentic workflow system for travel operators handling refunds, ticket changes, booking issues, policy validation, approvals, tool execution, and AI-assisted workflows. It is not a chatbot, generic dashboard, developer tool, or AI research interface.

Required deliverables:

1. Benchmark report.
2. UI elimination report.
3. Unified design philosophy.
4. Screen-by-screen blueprint for Task Inbox, Task Workspace, Approval Review, Agent Run Timeline, and Technical Evidence.
5. Complete design system.
6. Component inventory.
7. Rules every future screen must follow.

Optimize for enterprise trust, clarity, speed, accessibility, and daily operational efficiency. Avoid glassmorphism, cyberpunk aesthetics, futuristic AI dashboards, and decorative concept work.
